Scientific Proof

1. Rice Emits Biological Gas

Controlled experiments show rice grains emit trace amounts of CO2 when soaked, similar to metabolic processes in living organisms—an undeniable sign of life and bioactivity.

2. Rice Responds to Stimuli

Rice grains change their starch structure when exposed to specific frequencies of light and sound, resembling reactionary behavior typical of microscopic life forms.

3. Hidden Cellular Complexity

Advanced microscopy reveals cellular compartmentalization inside rice grains, displaying structural complexity beyond typical plant seeds—comparable to embryonic animal cells.
